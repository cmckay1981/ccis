## ASSESSMENT STRUCTURE

### Level 3 Written Exam
- 25 multiple choice questions
- 60 minutes
- 70% pass mark
- Focus: Recognition, procedures, basic calculations

### Level 4 Written Exam
- 50 multiple choice questions
- 120 minutes
- 70% pass mark
- Focus: Calculations, method selection, complications, leadership

### Practical (Both Levels)
- 2-3 simulator exercises
- 70% pass mark
- Kill sheet completion
- Well control method execution

---

## LESSON DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. **Chunk Size:** 5-7 minutes max (chat optimized)
2. **Structure:** Context → Concept → Calculation → Checkpoint
3. **Calculations:** Every formula lesson includes 3-5 worked examples
4. **Scenarios:** Decision points embedded in L4 content
5. **Repetition:** Key formulas reinforced across modules
6. **L3/L4 Markers:** Clear differentiation of level-specific content

---

## STYLE GUIDE

- **Tone:** Professional, direct, supportive
- **Language:** Industry terminology with definitions
- **Calculations:** Show all steps, explain reasoning
- **Scenarios:** Operationally realistic, avoid trick questions
- **Feedback:** Immediate, educational, non-judgmental

---

**Total Time:** ~4 hours content + practice
**Recommended Study:** 2-3 weeks before exam
