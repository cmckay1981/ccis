# Teamwork Masterclass (TW)

## Course Overview
A comprehensive course on building and maintaining high-performance teams in operational environments. Covers team dynamics, roles, coordination, conflict management, and team resilience.

## Course Structure
- **Course ID:** TW
- **Total Modules:** 6
- **Total Lessons:** 24
- **Duration:** 4 Training Days

## Modules

### TW-101: Team Fundamentals
Understanding what makes a team effective, including team formation, development stages, and characteristics of high-performing teams.

### TW-102: Roles and Responsibilities
Defining clear roles, managing responsibilities, and ensuring accountability within operational teams.

### TW-103: Team Coordination
Synchronizing team efforts, managing workload distribution, and maintaining shared situational awareness.

### TW-104: Trust and Psychological Safety
Building the foundation of effective teams through trust, psychological safety, and mutual respect.

### TW-105: Conflict and Resolution
Understanding team conflict, managing disagreements constructively, and turning conflict into improved outcomes.

### TW-106: Team Resilience
Building teams that adapt, recover from setbacks, and continuously improve performance under challenging conditions.
