# Communication Masterclass (COMM)

## Course Overview
A comprehensive course on operational communication in high-reliability environments. Covers communication fundamentals, active listening, closed-loop protocols, briefings, assertive communication, and adapting to different contexts.

## Course Structure
- **Course ID:** COMM
- **Total Modules:** 6
- **Total Lessons:** 24
- **Duration:** 4 Training Days

## Modules

### COMM-101: Communication Fundamentals
Core principles of effective operational communication including the communication model, clarity, brevity, and standard terminology.

### COMM-102: Active Listening
Developing listening skills that ensure accurate message receipt, including barriers to listening and demonstrating attention.

### COMM-103: Closed-Loop Communication
Implementing verification protocols including three-way communication, effective readbacks, and error correction.

### COMM-104: Briefings and Handovers
Structured communication for work transitions including pre-job briefings, shift handovers, and documentation.

### COMM-105: Assertive Communication
Speaking up effectively about concerns, graded assertiveness, and creating environments where people feel safe to communicate.

### COMM-106: Communication Context
Adapting communication to different situations including high-stress, cross-cultural, and technology-mediated contexts.
