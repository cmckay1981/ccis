## Module Structure

1. **SA-101: Foundations of Situational Awareness** (4 lessons)
   - What SA actually is, the three levels, SA degradation, why SA fails

2. **SA-102: Perception and Scanning** (4 lessons)
   - Active vs passive scanning, scan patterns, detecting change, perceptual limitations

3. **SA-103: Comprehension and Mental Models** (4 lessons)
   - Mental models, context effects, updating models, pattern recognition

4. **SA-104: Projection and Anticipation** (4 lessons)
   - Thinking ahead, trend recognition, mental simulation, decision triggers

5. **SA-105: Threats to SA** (4 lessons)
   - Distraction, fatigue, complacency, task saturation

6. **SA-106: Team Situational Awareness** (4 lessons)
   - Shared SA, communicating SA, cross-monitoring, handovers

## Key Learning Objectives
- Understand and apply the three-level SA model
- Develop effective perception and scanning habits
- Build accurate mental models and update them appropriately
- Project future states and establish decision triggers
- Recognize and mitigate SA threats
- Maintain shared SA across teams
