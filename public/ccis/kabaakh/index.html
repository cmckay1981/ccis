The Three Layers of the Mind

Long before psychology existed, the Egyptian mind was understood in three layers — each one shaping how you think, feel, and move through the world.

The Conscious Mind

The Thinker. The surface voice — logic, planning, analyzing, worrying. The loudest, but the least powerful.

The Subconscious Mind

The Keeper. Your emotional archive — patterns, attachment, reflexes. Where you react before you understand why.

The Super-Conscious Mind

The Higher Witness. The vantage point above emotion and identity. Where clarity feels obvious — and where manifestation becomes real.

KA.BA.AKH guides you through these layers in the correct order:
Body → Truth → Vision
